sambousik

our recipe from the middle east especially syria where there called burak

it is considered as the basic dish in ramadan

to prepare sambousik follow the steps below

  1. the dough ingredients:
    • oil
    • flour
    • sugar
    • salt
  2. the filling ingredients :
    • minced meat
    • pepper
    • onion
    • salt

now the steps

  1. mix the dough ingredients and let it cool for 15 min
  2. put the meat in frying pan
  3. add the salt pepper peaces of onion
  4. Keep stirring the meat until it is cooked
  5. let the meet chill
  6. Roll out the dough and place it in the mold
  7. fill it with the filling, but not too much
  8. In a deep bowl, put the frying oil, let it heat up and start frying
