carawyah podding

our ricepe is from syria but the main ingredient is from india which called carawya

the syrians used to introduce carawya as hospitality at the birth of a new baby or in cold weather

know lets start our delicious recipe

  • carwaya powder
  • rice powder
  • assorted nuts (pistachios, pine nuts, walnuts, etc.)
  • sugar
  • coconut flakes
  1. In a deep saucepan over heat, add all ingredients except the coconut and nuts.
  2. Continue stirring until you get a thick consistency and it starts to boil
  3. Then pour the hot caraway into yogurt cups or serving glasses
  4. Garnish with sprinkles of coconut and nuts.
