PIZZA

pizza recipe with simple ingredients

first let's talk about our recipe and how it is so simple ,fast and delicious

it is an Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped

with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients,baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

now lets talk about the ingredients:

  • the dough ingredients:
    • 2 cups of flour
    • pinch of sugar and salt
    • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
    • t s yeast
    • 1 cup of warm water
  • the sous ingredients:
    • tow tomatoes
    • olive oil
    • small onion
    • salt
    • sugar
    • garlic
    • pepper (spicey)
the steps
  1. the dough steps
    1. put the yeast in the warm water with the sugar while we mix the flour oil salt
    2. add the water and mix let it cool for 30 min
  2. the sous steps
    1. in the mixer put the tomatoes and mix it until we git smoothy tomato juice
    2. then In a saucepan, put the oil and saute the onion and garlic then add the juice
    3. add the sugar ,salt,and pinch of Wild thyme
  3. Spread the dough on a buttered baking tray
  4. Pour the sauce over the dough
  5. Garnish with grated cheddar cheese, olives, and green pepper slices
  6. You can sprinkle with oregano
  7. Place it in a preheated oven for 15 minutes.
